This RPGLE code is designed to retrieve and display the type and price of a product based on a given item number. 
The 'PRODUCTPF' file is used to store product information, including type and price details. 

The code fetches the relevant data from this file, ensuring that accurate product information is available for business operations such as inventory management, pricing analysis, or customer inquiries. 

This helps in maintaining up-to-date and accessible product data.
